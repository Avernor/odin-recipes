Chicken

Help

Take your chicken breast and garnish it with whatever the mundo you like, throw it in the pan at around 70% heat. Turn it after 1 minute. Throw a bit of butter in the pan. Turn it again after 1 minute. Then put the lid on the wait. After 20 minutes turn down the heat but leave it on the stove. Done.

Ingredients

  • Chicken 250g
  • Butter 100g
  • Rice 100g
  • Frozen Vegetables 80g

Steps

  1. Garnish chicken and throw in pan.
  2. After 1 minute turn it and add the butter.
  3. During the minute you are waiting, boil some water and add the rice and knor juice.
  4. Turn the chicken again and close the lid.
  5. Wait 20 minutes, turn off the heat.
  6. Serve. Bon appetit.
